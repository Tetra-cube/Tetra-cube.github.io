D&D 5e Content Reference

There's a lot of content for this game spread out across a bunch of different rulebooks. I made a quick reference to keep track of all the content that pertains to player characters, mostly for purposes of character creation.

Rulebooks

  • Player's Handbook
  • Elemental Evil Player's Companion
  • Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide
  • Volo's Guide to Monsters
  • Xanathar's Guide to Everything
  • The Tortle Package, One Grung Above, and Blood Hunter Class are extremely short books detailing a single race/class each.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide has a few options for player characters that require DM approval.
  • Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes is an upcoming book scheduled for release in May.
  • Unearthed Arcana is not a rulebook, but playtest material found on Wizards of the Coast's website. This content may be unbalanced, be careful when using it.
Spells can be found in the Player's Handbook, Elemental Evil Player's Companion, Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide, and Xanathar's Guide to Everything.

Feats can be found in the Player's Handbook, and Xanathar's Guide to Everything contains race-specific feats.

Classes
