D&D 5e Content Reference

There's a lot of content for this game spread out across a bunch of different rulebooks. I made a quick reference to keep track of all the content that pertains to player characters, mostly for purposes of character creation.

Rulebooks

  • Player's Handbook is the corebook which contains all of the base content.
  • Elemental Evil Player's Companion is a short online supplement, containing a few races and some spells.
  • Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide is primarily about the Sword Coast setting, but contains new subraces, subclasses, backgrounds, and a couple of spells.
  • Volo's Guide to Monsters contains 13(!) additional races, including playable monster races.
  • Xanathar's Guide to Everything has a tremendous amount of new subclasses, as well as spells and racial feats.
  • Dungeon Master's Guide has a few options for player characters that require DM approval.
  • The Tortle Package contains the Tortle race, and Blood Hunter Class contains the Blood Hunter, the only official class outside of the Player's Handbook.
  • Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes is an upcoming book scheduled for release in May.
  • Unearthed Arcana is not a rulebook, but playtest material found on the Wizards of the Coast website. This content may be unbalanced, be careful when using it.

  • Bugbear (Volo's Guide to Monsters)
  • Changeling (Unearthed Arcana: Eberron)
  • Dragonborn (Player's Handbook)
    • Draconic Ancestry:
      • Black, Blue, Brass, Bronze, Copper, Gold, Green, Red, Silver, White (Player's Handbook)
  • Dwarf (Player's Handbook)
    • Subraces:
      • Hill Dwarf, Mountain Dwarf (Player's Handbook)
      • Gray Dwarf/Duergar (Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide)
      • Gold Dwarf, Shield Dwarf (Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide)
        • Racial traits are identical to hill dwarves and mountain dwarves, respectively.
  • Elf (Player's Handbook)
    • Subraces:
      • High Elf, Wood Elf, Drow/Dark Elf (Player's Handbook)
      • Moon Elves, Sun Elves (Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide)
        • Racial traits are identical to high elves.
      • Avariel, Lythari, Sea Elves, Star Elves, Wild Elves (Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide)
        • No racial traits are provided for these elves (save Avariel, see below), so you will need to homebrew some racial traits if you want to play one of these subraces.
      • Eladrin(DMG) (Dungeon Master's Guide)
      • Eladrin(UA) (Unearthed Arcana: Eladrin and Gith)
      • Avariel, Grugach, Sea Elf, Shadar-kai (Unearthed Arcana: Elf Subraces)
  • Firbolg (Volo's Guide to Monsters)
  • Genasi (Elemental Evil Player's Companion)
    • Subraces:
      • Air Genasi, Earth Genasi, Fire Genasi, Water Genasi (Elemental Evil Player's Companion)
  • Gith (Unearthed Arcana: Eladrin and Gith)

      • Githyanki, Githzerai (Unearthed Arcana: Eladrin and Gith)

  • Goliath (Elemental Evil Player's Companion, Volo's Guide to Monsters)

Classes
